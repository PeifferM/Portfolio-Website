About Me

Who am I?

Originated, born and raised in the Rheinland. As a bilingual child of German-British parents growing up in the 90s I spent my childhood in Emmerich am Rhein near the Dutch Border. Most days were spent building tree huts in the forest, exploring ruines, construction sites and playgrounds. During this time period I drew a lot: mainly knights, dragons or creating my own football teams, leagues and kits in form of a magazine. After receiving my high school diploma in 2004 I spent a year abroad as an international student at the Nepean High School in Ottawa, Canada. Ties and holidays to Ottawa were already well-established as my grandmother emigrated to Canada from the UK at the start of WWII. It was here where I first made contact with concepts of Webdesign during a course focused on HTML layouts and Flash animation. I also attended art classes where I was able to dive into different painting and modulling techniques. After returning to Germany I moved to my father in Hoexter. I finished my technical diploma in business and administration in 2008. After a very eventul and memorable year as a community worker in a facility for people with learning dissabilities in Blairdrummond, Scotland I studied International Business in Bernburg, Saxony-Anhalt, which I finished with a bachelors degree in 2014 as well as with a degree in marketing at the Cape Peninsula University of Technology in Cape Town, South Africa, during my two semesters abroad in 2013. During my specialization in marketing I especially found a liking to Marketing Research and Brand identity. In 2014 however my father was suffering from bone cancer, which had already been diagnosed in 2012 but was enhancingly progressing. It was important for me to spend time and take care of him. He passed away in 2015 and for me a difficult and challenging time began. After being in a deadend for nearly three years I started an education in IT-sales for a medical software house which I completed in 2019. In class I worked on a project regarding the schools website, its status and ways of improvement. After some work and travel in Middlesbrough, UK I returend to Germany. In 2021 and 2022 I worked in the Covid-Pandemic preventation: as a helper in a vaccination center in Holzminden and in the health department covid hotline in Hoexter. I moved to Cologne in 2021 and as of October 2022 and after a time of self-reflection I am constantly trying to learn, educate myself and grow...in the field of Webdesign, UX-UI design and front-end development!

Motivation

Throughot my life I always enjoyed being creative. For me it was a long journey finding myself. All my past experiences led me here. I guess in the past I always focused on qualifying myself in general in order to keep the doors open to more job opportunities. I guess one of many key moments to starting a career in webdesign was when I was on vacation to Barcelona shortly after my job in the health department nexpired in November 2022. Through the many art exhibitions, architecture and lifestyle I was inspired to create a scrapbook from my travel. Ultimately I think in each lifecycle, stage I was surrounded by creativity and the fascination for it. Drawing, painting, visiting the national art gallery in Ottawa, cafes highlighting local art, art collections from artists modern and past and much more. To summarize: creating, making things look nice, clean and functional, yes that is Webdesign for me and that is me!
